Privacy Policy of Wasuramoti for Android

[Japanese] [TOP] [Wasuramoti]

Prohibition of sending personal information without user consent

This app does not transmit any personal information over the network, except when sending emails via "Support Form".

Personal information sent only for support purposes

When a user sends an email via "Support Form", the app sends the following useful debugging information to app developer. However, crucial information that could identify individuals, such as phone numbers, and any unnecessary information for debugging, will not be sent.
  • Device model name
  • Device's Android version
  • Wasuramoti's app version
  • Wasuramoti's settings

Prohibition of providing information to third parties

All information sent for support will not be given to any third party.
